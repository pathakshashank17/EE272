Vmpp = Vin = 29 V
Impp = Iin = 7.35 A
Rmpp = 3.94 Ohm
Pin = 213.15 W = Pout

Ro (< Rmpp for buck) = 1 Ohm
Vo = (Pin * Ro)^0.5 = 14.599 V
Io = Pin/Vo = 14.6 A
D = Vo/Vin = 14.599/29 = 0.503 i.e. 50.3%

Let f = 50000 Hz, max ripple = 10%
L = (Vin - Vo) * D/(f * delIo) = 9.92e-5 H
C = D * (1 - D) * Vin/(8 * f^2 * L * delVo) = 2.502e-6 F
